Reflection

Being a graduate student at Mizzou through the Learning Technologies and Design program has been a great experience. There are many skills that I have learned and I expect to use in my professional career. I have gained experienced in each of the core competencies emphasized in this program.

Core Competencies of the University of Missouri Learning Technologies and Design

To practice first core competency, Design and Development of learning systems, my classes allowed me to conduct research on the different areas of learning technologies and design. I learned about the different systems of instructional design and used rapid tools to design learning. I explored Canvas as an instructor and learned the importance of web design and development. The second core competency, Front-End Analysis and System Evaluation, was demonstrated through evaluations of the learning systems in all of my classes using a very hands-on process. This allowed me to test my learning systems with different users. Lastly, in order to practice Leadership and Social Responsibility, I was able to interact with my peers respectfully through discussion boards and video conferencing. In my research course, I learned the importance of following a code of ethics. In my digital media course, we demonstrated the importance of giving credit to the owners of any digital media that we use.
