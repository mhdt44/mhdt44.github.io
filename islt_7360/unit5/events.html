Upcoming Events!

  • Back to School Drive - (August 5-9) The back to school drive donations will go to students who are in need of school supplies.
  • Ball4MD - (September14 and 15) Usually held in September, Ball4MD is a basketball tournament held to spread awareness of Muscular Dystrophy.
  • Glow Walk, Run, Wheel - (December 23) The Glow Walk, Run, Wheel is an annual event and the route for the walk is Tinian's first-ever wheelchair-accesible route.
  • International Day of Acceptance - (January 20) Throughout the month of January, we sell ribbons for $1. Each ribbon represents a different kind of disability.
