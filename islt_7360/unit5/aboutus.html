Welcome to Team Koka's Website!

Established in 2012, Team Koka is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to promoting awareness of different disabilities in the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, specifically the tiny island of Tinian. Team Koka is comprised of three sisters: Martina, Mikaela, and Monika Diaz.

Members

Monika Diaz, President

Monika is the original founding member of Team Koka. She currently works for the Department of Youth Services on Tinian.

Mikaela Diaz, Vice-President

Mikaela Diaz has been a member of Team Koka since it was created. She currently works as a Special Education Classroom Teacher at Tinian Jr. Sr. High School.

Martina Diaz, Member

Martina has been a member of Teak Koka since it was created. She currently works at Tinian Jr. Sr. High School as the Vice-Principal.
