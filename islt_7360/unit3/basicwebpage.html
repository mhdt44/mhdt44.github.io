Martina's Basic Webpage

For this project, I'm going to focus on a non-profit organization called Team Koka.

Team Koka was founded in 2012 by my sisters and I. We wanted to start a non-profit organization to host events in our community that promoted awareness of the different disabilities in our community.

Topic #1

For topic #1, I will discuss the purpose of Team Koka and how we were created. I'll also discuss the motivation behind forming the organization. I will also discuss and post pictures of the different events we have hosted. I would like to include different ways people could get involved and perhaps include a list of other community organizations they could get involved with as well.

Upcoming Events

  • Back to School Drive - (August) The back to school drive donations will go to students who are in need of school supplies.
  • Ball4MD - (September) Usually held in September, Ball4MD is a basketball tournament held to spread awareness of Muscular Dystrophy.
  • Glow Walk, Run, Wheel - (December) The Glow Walk, Run, Wheel is an annual event and the route for the walk is Tinian's first-ever wheelchair-accesible route.
  • International Day of Acceptance - (January) Throughout the month of January, we sell ribbons for $1. Each ribbon represents a different kind of disability.

Ways to get involved

  1. Participate in events
  2. Volunteer at events
  3. Make a donation
